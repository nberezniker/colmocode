description: Generates new complete sub-agent configurations or Antigravity workflows.
---

This workflow acts as a meta-generator to expand the IDE's capabilities.

### Steps:

1. **Design**:
   - Understand the desired persona or capability from the user.
   - Define the necessary tools and instructions.

2. **Generation**:
   - Format the new capability as a `.md` workflow file in `.agents/workflows/` or as a new skill.
   - Use the established Antigravity Markdown standards and YAML frontmatter.

3. **Cleanup**:
   - Ensure the new agent/workflow doesn't overlap excessively with existing ones.
   - Validate that it follows the **Senior Architect** tone.
